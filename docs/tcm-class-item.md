# Timeless Chronomancer (Class) (AC)

**Weapon Damage:** 100%, 2.0 speed  

**Description:**  
Recommended Enhancements: Lucky, Hybrid. Timeless Chronomancers rely on Hourglasses and Corruption they create using their Hourglass Pet (equipable items found in the shop via your Timeless Hourglass Pet) to manipulate and distort time itself.

**Stat Model:** Full Hybrid  

**Special Effects:**

- Corrupted Chronomancers gain mana from all hits landed in combat, and especially on critical hits.
- The amount depends on damage relative to their own HP total.

---

## Skills

### Corrupted Sand Strike

- **Rank Needed:** 0  
- **Mana Cost:** 0 Mana  
- **Cooldown:** 2 Seconds  
- **Type:** Physical  
- **Range:** A short range attack that deals damage to 1 target.  

**Description:**  
Deals damage based on your weapon damage. Each of your skills gets an additional benefit from hourglasses and corruption you equip in your consumables slot. Hourglasses and corruption can be obtained from the Hourglass Pet available from the Book of Lore.

**Notes:**

- If Infinite Corruption is active, applies **Infinity Rend** for 40 seconds, a DoT that stacks to 15.
- If Timepiece of Madness is active, applies the following effects:
  - Applies one stack of **Mad** for every use of Corrupted Sand Strike.
  - At 5 stacks of Mad, the next Corrupted Sand Strike consumes these stacks and applies **Eternal**.
  - Eternal boosts your critical strike chance by 80% and reduces the damage you deal by 30%.

---

### Sand Rift

- **Rank Needed:** 1  
- **Mana Cost:** 15 Mana  
- **Cooldown:** 2.5 Seconds  
- **Type:** Hybrid  
- **Range:** A short range attack that deals damage to 1 target.  

**Description:**  
Deals damage to your target and applies **Temporal Rift** to you. Temporal Rift stacks 4 times, lasts 30 seconds and is used by your other abilities.

---

### Hourglass Inversion

- **Rank Needed:** 2  
- **Mana Cost:** 30 Mana  
- **Cooldown:** 8 Seconds  
- **Type:** Hybrid  
- **Range:** An infinite range skill that heals yourself.  

**Description:**  
Reverses time to heal you. Heal is increased by stacks of Temporal Rift and consumes those stacks.

**Notes:**

- If Hourglass of Power is active, applies **Power** for 30 seconds, increasing damage, haste, crit chance, crit damage, hit chance, dodge chance and damage reduction by 10%.

---

### Corruption Through Time

- **Rank Needed:** 3  
- **Mana Cost:** 20 Mana  
- **Cooldown:** 6 Seconds  
- **Type:** Hybrid  
- **Range:** A long range status skill that applies to 1 target.  

**Description:**  
Applies **Corruption Through Time**. Corruption Through Time increases all damage you do by 20% for 10 seconds. Doesn’t stack.

**Notes:**

- If Entropic Corruption is active, applies the following effects:
  - **Entropic Harm** for 10 seconds, increasing damage taken by 150%.
  - **Entropic Mana** for 7 seconds, increasing haste by 30% and reducing mana costs by 50%.
  - **Entropic Power** for 4 seconds, increasing damage by 100%.
  - **Focus** for 3 seconds, forcing affected monsters to attack you.

- If Foresee Corruption is active, applies the following effects:
  - **Foresee** for 150 seconds.
  - Using Foresee Corruption again replaces this effect with **Seer** for 20 seconds.
  - Using Corruption Through Time again replaces Seer with **Change the Future**.
  - Change the Future applies Focus to enemies, and decreases damage taken by 500%, and increases dodge chance, haste, hit chance, damage, and crit damage by 100% for 5 seconds.

- If Hourglass of Paradise is active, applies the following effects (or removes them if they are currently active):
  - **Welcome to Paradise**, reducing damage taken by 60% and increasing haste by 50%. In addition, provides a Heal over Time that also regenerates mana. Lasts 2 hours.
  - **Price of Paradise**, reducing crit chance and outgoing damage by 30%. Lasts 2 hours.

- If Hourglass of Transience is active, applies the following effects:
  - **Transient** for 10 seconds, a self DoT that increases in strength.
  - Using Corruption Through Time again replaces Transient with **Ephemeral** for 5 seconds, increasing dodge and damage by +50%.

- If Timepiece of Madness is active, applies the following effects:
  - Applies **Potential**, increasing your Hit Chance by 20% for 20 seconds. Cooldown: 12 seconds.

- If Timepiece of Inevitability is active, applies the following effects:
  - Applies **Inevitable Corruption**, increasing your outgoing damage and haste by 12% for 20 seconds.
  - Using the Timepiece of Inevitability while Inevitable Corruption is active applies **Snap** to your opponent, dealing significant damage to them after a short delay. Cooldown: 20 seconds.

- If Timepiece of Pestilence is active, applies the following effects:
  - Applies **Infectious Air**, increasing the amount of damage the enemy takes by 20% for 10 seconds, stacking to 3 but is consumed by Temporal Collapse.
  - Your incoming damage is increased by 100% for 10 seconds.
  - Applies a light DoT to your opponent.
  - Applies **Decay** to your opponent, preventing them from healing.

# Timeless Chronomancer Class Items

## Entropic Corruption

[Corrupted Chronomancer Class] For the next 30 seconds, Corruption Through Time also reduces mana costs by 50% and increases Haste by 30% for 7 seconds. It also increases outgoing damage by 100% for 4 seconds and increases damage taken by 150% for 10 seconds. Finally it applies Focus to your target, forcing them to attack you for 3 seconds. Cooldown: 6 seconds.

---

## Foresee Corruption

[Corrupted Chronomancer Class] For the next 10 seconds, Corruption Through Time also applies Foresee for 150 seconds. Using this Corruption while Foresee is active will consume it and apply Seer for 20 seconds. Using Corruption Through Time while Seer is active removes it and applies Change the Future, reducing your damage taken by 80% and increasing Dodge Chance, Haste, Hit Chance, Critical Strike Damage, and Outgoing Damage by 100% for 5 seconds. Cooldown: 60 seconds.

---

## Infinite Corruption

[Corrupted Chronomancer Class] For the next 30 seconds, Corrupted Sand Strike applies a DoT that stacks up to 15 times and lasts 40 seconds. Your Hourglass Inversion skill also applies a Heal over Time that lasts for 20 seconds and doesn't stack. Cooldown: 6 seconds.

---

## Hourglass of Paradise (1)

[Corrupted Chronomancer Class] For the next 2 hours, Corruption Through Time applies Welcome to Paradise, which reduces your damage taken by 60%, increases your Haste by 50%, applies a heal and mana over time, but reduces your outgoing damage and Critical Strike Chance by 30%. Using Corruption Through Time while Welcome to Paradise is active toggles it off.

---

## Hourglass of Power (1)

[Corrupted Chronomancer Class] For the next 2 hours, Hourglass Inversion applies Power, increasing your outgoing damage, Haste, Critical Strike Chance, Critical Strike Damage, Hit Chance, Dodge Chance, and reducing your damage taken by 10% for 30 seconds. Does not stack.

---

## Hourglass of Transience (1)

[Corrupted Chronomancer Class] For the next 2 hours, Corruption Through Time applies Transient, a growing DoT to you for 20 seconds. While Transient it applies Ephemeral, increasing Dodge and damage by 50% for 5 seconds. Using Hourglass Inversion while Transient or Ephemeral stabilizes you, removing them and applying Incorporeal, increasing END and Haste by 50% for 10 seconds. While Transient, Sand Rift applies Flow, increasing enemy damage taken by 8% for 5 seconds, stacking up to 3. While Stable it applies Ebb, a growing DoT for 8 seconds.
